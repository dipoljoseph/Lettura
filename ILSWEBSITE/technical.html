Technical Report

Is a document that needs different approaches of data collection and treatment.

Parts of a Technical Report

Title Page - Includes the report in title, your name, and the submission date.

Executive Summary - This is the overview of subject matter.

Table of Contents - List of numbered sections in report and their page number.

Introduction - Includes terms of reference abd the outline of reports structure.

Body - Includes headings and sub-headings.

Conclusion - States the major interferences.

Recommendations - Indicates any further work that needs to be done.

Reference List - This is the list of reference material consulted.

Appendix - Contains information that supports your analysis, but is not essential to its explanation.
