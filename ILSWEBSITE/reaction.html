Reaction Paper

Is a form of paper writing (an academic essay) in which the writer expresses his/her ideas and opinions about what has been seen or read.

Parts of a Reaction Paper

Introduction - Summarizes the Story

Body

Conclusion - Summarizes the body and includes recommendations/suggestions

Steps in Writing a Reaction Paper

1. Read or watch and respond

2. Draft

3. State your opinion

4. Summarize and conclude
